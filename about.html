About Us

Welcome to the Learning Management System (LMS) Project. Our platform is designed to facilitate online learning and management for educational institutions.

Features include:

  • User management for students, trainers, and admins
  • Course creation and enrollment
  • Live and recorded lectures
  • Performance tracking and reporting
  • Compliance and system updates

Our mission is to provide a seamless and effective learning experience for all users.
